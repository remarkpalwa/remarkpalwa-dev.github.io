2020 AKLAN HAMMOCK FESTIVAL

If you ever decide to take a day off not to stay idle at home, to watch the sunset instead of Netflix, here’s one for you. 3rd Aklan Hammock Fest ( for the beneficiaries of Aklan Trekkers) is on February 22-23, 2020.

OBJECTIVES
  1. To connect, build, and strengthen camaraderie and friendship among organizations and individuals who love the outdoors.
  2. To contribute to the development and promotion of local and national tourism coupled with advocacy on environmental protection and conservation as well as reaching out to remote and indigenous communities.
  3. To raise fund for Aklan Trekkers ‘Rebuild A House Project’ for Typhoon Ursula Victims in Malay, Aklan and Upland Outreach Mission in Taroytoy Elementary School, Sitio Taroytoy, Brgy. Manika, Libacao, Aklan in March.
  4. To support local outdoor products by promoting our brand partners and sponsors.

2023 AKLAN HAMMOCK FESTIVAL

Dance and swing to the music of the Island Drumbeaters, Jed, Kevin & Angelo at the 5th Aklan Hammock Festival’s Musikahan.

As our opening act, we have Maura of the Phoenix Firedancers to heat up the night.🔥 We’ll also have raffle draws for the Musikahan ticket holders. 🎟

You can get your tickets at KAPE MEN and Quick Wash Laundry in Ibajay or just message us if interested. Part of the ticket sales will go to our beneficiaries. Gates open at 6, see you there!!🌟
